About Us


M/S ALLAHABAD SILICA SAND INDUSTRIES is a market giant which has established
itself as a Manufacturer and Supplier of Silica Sand. Based in Allahabad, Uttar Pradesh,
the company has been covering various milestones of success under the proficient
headship of the Proprietor Mr.Shubham Pandey .He has vast industry experience and
great knowledge with which he has been guiding us to scale new heights of success.

Team:We are backed by a strong team which acts as the backbone of the organization.
Our team is comprised of skilled professionals, quality Controllers, Marketing Personnel etc.
These individuals have profound experience and work in complete coordination with one another
to produce the best quality Silica Sand.

Quality Assurance:Quality is the main forte of our firm and we always remain on our
toes to maintain it. We follow specialized technique in the processing of our Silica Sand to
ensure best quality. Silica Sand is processed by the industry experts who make sure that it
is free from impurities. Shankargarh Silica Sand is well recognized in the market for is
purity as well as other attributes.

Customer Satisfaction:Our clients are most important for us and we make sure that
they are delivered ultimate customer satisfaction. In order to achieve this goal, we ensure
that only the best quality Silica Sand is offered to them.We offer Silica Sand right as per
their specifications. We always welcome the feedback received from the clients end to keep
on improving ourselves.
